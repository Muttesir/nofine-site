HOW IT WORKS

Three steps,
zero fines.

01
📍

GPS Detects You

As soon as you cross into a drop-off zone, NoFine fires a notification — even if the app is closed or your phone is locked.

02
✅

Confirm in One Tap

Tap YES on the notification. A charge card is created instantly with the fee, deadline, and a direct Pay Now link.

03
💳

Pay Before Midnight

Smart reminders count down to the midnight deadline. Tap Pay Now to open the airport's payment page with your plate pre-copied.

£160
Maximum PCN fine

Airport drop-off zones charge £7–£10 per entry. If you forget to pay, a Penalty Charge Notice arrives in the post — up to £160 at Heathrow, Gatwick, and Stansted. NoFine ensures you never get caught out.
