Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED · MAY 2026

NoFine ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

  • Vehicle registration plate — used to identify your vehicle and look up vehicle details via the DVLA API.
  • Your name — stored locally on your device and on our servers to personalise your experience.
  • Location data — used in the background to detect when you enter airport drop-off zones. Location data is processed on-device and is never sent to our servers.
  • Push notification token — used to send payment reminders to your device.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To detect airport drop-off zone entries and create charge reminders.
  • To send push notifications reminding you to pay charges before the deadline.
  • To look up vehicle details using your registration plate via the DVLA API.

DATA STORAGE

Your name, vehicle plate, and push notification token are stored on our secure servers hosted on Railway (US West). All other data (charges, payment history, settings) is stored locally on your device only.

LOCATION DATA

NoFine uses background location to detect when you enter airport drop-off zones. This data is processed entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your location to our servers.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

  • DVLA API — vehicle registration lookup (gov.uk)
  • Expo — push notification delivery
  • Railway — server hosting

DATA RETENTION

Your data is retained for as long as you use the app. You can delete all your data at any time using the "Reset & Start Over" option in Settings.

YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To request data deletion, contact us at support@nofine.app.

CONTACT

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@nofine.app.
