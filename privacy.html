Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED · MAY 2026

NoFine ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

  • Vehicle registration plate — used to identify your vehicle and look up details via the DVLA API.
  • Your name — stored locally on your device and on our servers to personalise your experience.
  • Location data — used in the background to detect when you enter airport drop-off zones. Location data is processed entirely on-device and is never sent to our servers.
  • Push notification token — used to send payment reminders to your device.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To detect airport drop-off zone entries and create charge reminders.
  • To send push notifications reminding you to pay charges before the deadline.
  • To look up vehicle details using your registration plate via the DVLA API.

LOCATION DATA

NoFine uses background location access to detect when you enter airport drop-off zones. This data is processed entirely on your device using iOS geofencing. We do not collect, store, or transmit your location to our servers at any point.

DATA STORAGE

Your name, vehicle plate, and push notification token are stored on our secure servers hosted on Railway (US West). All other data — including charges, payment history, and app settings — is stored locally on your device only and never leaves it.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

  • DVLA API — vehicle registration lookup (gov.uk)
  • Expo — push notification delivery infrastructure
  • Railway — secure server hosting

DATA RETENTION

Your data is retained for as long as you use the app. You can delete all your data at any time by tapping "Reset & Start Over" in the Settings screen.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

NoFine is intended for use by professional drivers aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

CONTACT & DATA REQUESTS

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To make a data request or ask any questions about this policy, contact us at the email below.
